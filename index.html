Welcome to my World!

I'm a web designer, programmer & digital illustrator by day & hunter for the perfect Sicilian slice by night (& most weekdays...and weekends too...) Based & raised in NYC, I have an extensive background in multiple design disciplines & love to merge digital with analog. I'm the founder of Lost Lagoon Loungewear & have a super cool side-kick pup, Ernesto!

Skills

Design & Development

My first introduction to HTML & CSS, like many in my generation, was through Myspace (Kudos to JankenPop for bringing back the party!) Though the internet has evolved since then & web design has become much more sophisticated, who doesn't still love a good glitter gif now and again?

In college I created & coded web based multimedia projects combining fiction with photography. Post college, I've worked as a VP of Fashion Design in NYC's Garment District, honing my Photoshop & Illustrator skills. Often in charge of designing & creating websites for brands, it was a great incubator for developing design & programming skills. This lead me to my true love--Web Design & Development: a perfect synergy of problem solving, trend analysis, good design & functionality.

Illustration

I began oil painting at 7 & have been creating ever since! My favorite mediums are acrylic paint, colored pencil, digital sketching & more often than not, a good ol' bic pen. My style is more abstract & whimsical (& sometimes just strange.) I believe that hand drawn illustrations can bring a warmth & joy often missing in digital media. My mission is to make the internet a more joyful place, one website at a time! You can find some of my artwork here.

Writing

Writing--espeically creatively--has always been a love of mine. I followed that passion to a BA in Creative Writing. It allows me to cleary communicate complex ideas in a way that is concise, easily understandable & engaging. From poetry to web copy-- I haven't met a word I didn't like (except for moist. Moist can go scratch.)
